Our Story

Founded in 2015 in Nairobi, Metal Art Designers began as a small workshop with a passion for blending metal and wood into functional art.

What started as a passion project has grown into a respected furniture design studio known for our innovative designs and exceptional craftsmanship.

Every piece we create tells a story of dedication, skill, and the perfect marriage of industrial and natural materials.
